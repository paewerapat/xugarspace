Resurs-P Type

Resurs-P represents the pinnacle of Earth observation technology, focusing on environmental monitoring, natural resource management, and comprehensive area tracking. Equipped with high-resolution imaging across multiple spectral bands including visible light, infrared, and near-infrared (NIR), it provides exceptional detail with 1-meter color resolution and 0.8-meter panchromatic resolution. This advanced capability supports precision agriculture assessment, topographic mapping, land use planning, and environmental monitoring with unprecedented accuracy.
